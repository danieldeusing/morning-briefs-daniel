CFTC COT (positioning report): A US agency publishes every Friday how many bets on rising vs falling Real rates large speculators hold on the Chicago futures exchange (as of Tuesday). Like a peek at the betting slips of pro traders: if you see many "Real rises" tickets and few "Real falls" tickets, smart money believes in a stable Real. "Net +71,651 long" means: 71,651 more contracts on up than on down — a clear pro-Real signal.

Carry trade (BRL): Borrow where rates are low (e.g. in euros) and lend in Brazil at 14.50 % Selic — the rate spread is your profit, as long as the Real stays stable. If the Real crashes hard, the currency loss eats the rate gain, and the party ends. That's why high Selic supports the Real — and that's exactly why today's "speculators stay long Real" finding matters: pro money believes the carry holds.
