SRAG & RSV: SRAG = "severe respiratory disease with hospital connection," Fiocruz counts weekly. RSV = Respiratory Syncytial Virus — for older kids mostly just a runny nose, for a 1-month-old dangerous because it can clog the tiny airways. Fever, > 60 breaths/min, rib retractions, blue lips or any fever under 3 months: go to ER immediately.
