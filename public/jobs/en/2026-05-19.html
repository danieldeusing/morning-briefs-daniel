Niche premium · Embedded cluster (four bullseyes)

Today's Tuesday spike delivers more embedded volume than the typical week's middle — four listings, all with a clearly defensible CV link. That's not typical; usually one to two embedded niche listings per week is the average. Daniel's pricing position: €120–135/h ask, €110/h floor.

U-Boot · Device tree · BSP: Picture an embedded system (semiconductor test board, car control unit) as a mini-computer without a screen or BIOS. U-Boot is the initial spark when power is applied — the first software that runs before the actual operating system boots. The device tree is the parts list that tells the Linux kernel which chips are on which pins (instead of compiling that into the kernel). The BSP (Board Support Package) is the custom-tailored stack of U-Boot, device tree, and kernel modules for exactly that one piece of hardware. Whoever masters this is the mechanic who gets the car running without the manual — rare and expensive.
