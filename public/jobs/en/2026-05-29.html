RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation): rather than letting the AI model hallucinate freely, the system first searches a document database for matching passages and feeds them to the model as context. Result: the model cites real sources instead of guessing. Exactly what Nemensis and Camunda occupy today — application-layer AI, not foundation-model training.
Agentic Automation (Camunda slot): workflows where LLM "agents" autonomously decide individual steps — e.g., a reimbursement request where the model checks, queries, escalates instead of hardcoding each step. Camunda combines this with BPMN, the classic diagram standard for business processes: deterministic steps stay modeled, fuzzy ones the agent handles.
