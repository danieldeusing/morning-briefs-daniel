More pronounced than yesterday, today its own applied-AI track on the freelance board has coalesced — and it fits twiceD's stated focus (LLM integration, RAG, agent tooling, not research). Top remains KMG Advisory (Java + GenAI, fit 87); behind it a Python/FastAPI field at fit 72–77: Nemensis (senior RAG engineer, Python/FastAPI/cloud, 100% remote), RED SAP Solutions (software AI engineer, Python/FastAPI, LangGraph/LangChain), thryve talent (applied AI engineer, LLM OpenAI/Claude/Gemini), BLUECHILLED (full stack AI, Python/FastAPI), HOSSMANN (enterprise RAG) and 10X Group (senior AI/LLM engineer, LangChain/LlamaIndex, remote CH/DE).

RAG (retrieval-augmented generation): Instead of letting the AI model make up the answer freely, the system first searches a document database for relevant passages and feeds them to the model as context — like a student quickly checking the textbook before answering. Result: verifiable answers instead of guesses. These roles demand exactly this pipeline work (vector DB, embeddings, LangChain/LlamaIndex) — close to Daniel's Python/FastAPI profile and to twiceD's AI focus.

This track seldom carries the premium band, but sits in the reached standard range (90–130 €/h*) and is strategically interesting: it aligns with the AI tooling twiceD builds anyway. Python/FastAPI, otherwise thin in the pure Java board, moves here prominently into view.
