Project Tenders (TED EU)

Public EU tenders — client is always public, requirements formally documented. Hence the partnership-gap analysis: twiceD is a solo/2-person team; high revenue, FTE, or certificate hurdles need a partner.

Partnership gap analysis: Public tenders require qualification proof (minimum revenue, team size, references, certificates). Where twiceD as a small team falls short, a partner fills the gap — like in house building, where the solo architect brings in a certified engineer for structural calcs. "✅ direct" = can bid solo; "⚠️ partner" = stack fits, but one mandatory hurdle is missing and specifically named.
