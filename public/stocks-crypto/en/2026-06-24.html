Micron reports tonight; until the numbers land, every price is just a bet on the outcome. A beat above consensus ($34.66B) plus strong AI-memory guidance drives it; a miss or cautious outlook punishes hard, especially after a +324 % YTD run.

Gold & Oil [9][10]

Gold ~$4,074 — Heading Toward 7-Month Low

Spot gold quoted on 06-24 at $4,074/oz (−$39.61, −0.96 %; TradingEconomics: $4,075.70) and slipping toward a 7-month low. Counterintuitively but logically: the market is pricing in tighter Fed policy — and higher real rates plus a firmer dollar are poison for a non-yielding asset. Iran peace optimism dampens geopolitical risk but doesn't support gold enough.[9]

Real rate: Nominal rate minus inflation. Gold pays no interest — when bonds suddenly yield more in real terms (because the Fed gets tighter), holding gold becomes relatively more expensive. So gold falls even though geopolitics remain nervous: right now, the direction of rates matters more than the level of risk.
